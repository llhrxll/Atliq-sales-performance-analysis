# 📊 AtliQ Sales Performance Analysis

An end-to-end Sales & Finance Analytics project built in **Microsoft Excel** using **Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX** to transform raw transactional data into interactive business intelligence dashboards.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales and financial performance for **AtliQ Hardware**, a company that sells computers, peripherals, and networking products across multiple countries through various retail partners.

The objective is to convert large volumes of raw sales data into meaningful insights that help business stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer performance
- Evaluate product performance
- Compare actual sales against targets
- Track market and regional sales performance
- Generate Profit & Loss reports
- Analyze Gross Margin (GM%) trends
- Build an interactive business dashboard

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Net Sales showed strong year-over-year growth across three fiscal years.
- India emerged as the highest revenue-generating market.
- The PC division achieved the highest YoY sales growth.
- Overall sales remained below the annual sales target, indicating opportunities for improvement.
- Gross Margin percentage declined as Cost of Goods Sold increased faster than revenue.
- Top customers contributed a significant portion of total sales.

## 📂 Data Model

The project follows a **Star Schema** data model.

### Fact Table
- fact_sales_monthly

### Dimension Tables
- dim_customer
- dim_product
- dim_market
- dim_date

### Supporting Table
- sales_targets

This structure enables efficient reporting and faster analytical calculations.
